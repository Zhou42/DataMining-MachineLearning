Transferring files back and forth to the VM

1) Change your network adapter to a Bridged Adaptor:

  • In Virtual Box, select Hadoop in the left sidebar
  • Click Settings at the top of the VirtualBox window

  • Click Network
  • Change "Attached to:" to "Bridged Adapter"

2) Run your Hadoop VM (by double clicking it in the left sidebar)

Now on your main VirtualBox Manager (similar to the first figure), double click on the Hadoop in order to run the Hadoop VM.
